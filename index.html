I'm Kez.

So what's up with this website? I created this as an experiment to explore even just a small tidbit of all the cool knowledge the world has to offer.
I have a problem with the concept of "studying." To me, "I'm studying" sounds like someone is painfully sticking their head in a book and mindlessly repeating facts that they think they'll find relevant in their future (and maybe add some groans and i've-to-clutch-my-head mindaches into the scenario). Sure, when there's a deadline or test around the corner, that kind of "studying" could end up being necessary. But this website isn't about "studying" knowledge. It's about bridling the untamed horse of information (pardon my cliche) and letting it take you on the wildest ride of your life.
I hope you'll join me on this ride, wherever it may take us.
